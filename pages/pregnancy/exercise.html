Trimester 1

Pregnancy Exercise for Trimester 1

During 1 - 12 Weeks, it's recommended to start with gentle exercises.

Breathing Movements: Do for 5 Minutes.

Trimester 2

Pregnancy Exercise for Trimester 2

During 13 - 28 Weeks, it's recommended to start with gentle exercises.

Perform Movements 1: Do repetition for 8 repetitions.
Perform Movements 2: Do repetition for 8 repetitions.
Perform Movements 3: Do repetition for 3 repetitions.
Perform Movements 4: Do repetition for 2 repetitions.
Perform Movements 5: Do repetition for 4 repetitions.
Perform Movements 6: Do repetition for 2 repetitions right & left.
Perform Movements 7: Breathing Movements for 8 repetitions.

Trimester 3

Pregnancy Exercise for Trimester 3

During 29 - 40 Weeks, it's recommended to start with gentle exercises.

Perform Movements 1:Do repetetition for 8 counts (right & left).
Perform Movements 2: Do repetition for 8 counts.
Perform Movements 3: Do repetition for 3 counts.
Perform Movements 4: Do repetetition for 8 counts (right & left).
Perform Movements 5: Do repetetition for 8 counts (right & left).
