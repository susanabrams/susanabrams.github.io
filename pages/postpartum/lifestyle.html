Healthy Lifestyle

Explore mom's,
a healthier lifestyle!

Postpartum period is a time for mothers to recover after childbirth. This period typically lasts for 6-8 weeks. Adopting a healthy lifestyle during the postpartum period is crucial to help the mother's body return to its pre-pregnancy condition and improve her physical and mental health.

Nutrition

Consume a balanced diet with adequate portions. Make sure to include plenty of fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and complex carbohydrates.

Hydration

Drink at least 8 glasses of water per day to keep your body hydrated and promote breast milk production.

Rest

Ensure to get enough sleep, at least 7-8 hours per day. Create a comfortable and quiet sleeping environment.

Exercise

Start with light exercise gradually after 2-3 weeks postpartum. Exercise can help strengthen your body muscles, improve mood, and increase blood circulation.

Protein is an essential nutrient during pregnancy as it helps in the formation of body tissues and fetal growth. Some good sources of protein for postpartum women include:

Skinless Chicken

Salmon

Nuts

Eggs

Carbohydrates provide essential energy for postpartum women. Healthy carbohydrate choices include:

Brown Rice

Potatoes

Cereals

Quinoa

Fiber helps maintain healthy digestion during the postpartum period. Some high-fiber foods recommended for postpartum women include:

Apples

Broccoli

Cashews

Flax Seeds

Vitamins and minerals are important for postpartum recovery and the health of new mothers. Some foods rich in vitamins and minerals include:

Oranges

Spinach

Milk

Sardines

In addition, postpartum mothers should also pay attention to the following:

  • Limit consumption of foods high in fat, sugar, and salt.
  • Consume foods that are cooked properly to avoid food poisoning.

By paying attention to these factors, postpartum mothers can better maintain their health and recovery after childbirth.
